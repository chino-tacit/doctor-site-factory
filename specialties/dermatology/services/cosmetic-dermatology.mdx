## Overview

Enhance your natural beauty with our suite of cosmetic dermatology services. Our board-certified dermatologists combine medical expertise with artistic vision to deliver natural-looking results that help you look as vibrant as you feel.

## Our Cosmetic Services

### Neuromodulators
- **Botox® / Dysport® / Xeomin®** - Smooth dynamic wrinkles (forehead, crow's feet, frown lines)
- **Preventative Botox** - Early intervention for younger patients
- **Masseter Botox** - Jaw slimming, TMJ relief

### Dermal Fillers
- **Juvéderm® / Restylane®** - Lip enhancement, cheek volume, nasolabial folds
- **Sculptra®** - Collagen stimulation for gradual volume restoration
- **Radiesse®** - Immediate volume with collagen stimulation

### Laser & Energy-Based
- **Fraxel®** - Fractional resurfacing for texture, scars, sun damage
- **IPL / BBL** - Pigmentation, redness, sun spots
- **Laser Hair Removal** - Permanent reduction for all skin types
- **Tattoo Removal** - PicoSure® technology

### Skin Rejuvenation
- **Chemical Peels** - VI Peel, TCA, glycolic for glow and texture
- **Microneedling** - Collagen induction with PRP option
- **HydraFacial®** - Multi-step hydration and extraction

Every cosmetic journey begins with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, assess your anatomy, and create a personalized treatment plan. We prioritize natural results and patient safety above all.
