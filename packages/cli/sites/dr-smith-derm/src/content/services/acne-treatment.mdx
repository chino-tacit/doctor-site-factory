## Overview

Acne affects millions of Americans of all ages. At City Dermatology Clinic, we understand that every patient's skin is unique, which is why we create personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific type of acne, skin type, and lifestyle.

## Treatment Options

- **Topical medications** - Retinoids, benzoyl peroxide, antibiotics
- **Oral medications** - Antibiotics, hormonal therapy, isotretinoin (Accutane)
- **Procedural treatments** - Chemical peels, laser therapy, extraction
- **Lifestyle guidance** - Skincare routine, diet considerations, stress management

## What to Expect

Your first visit includes a comprehensive skin evaluation, discussion of your medical history, and development of a customized treatment plan. Follow-up appointments monitor progress and adjust treatments as needed.

Most patients see improvement within 6-8 weeks of starting treatment. We're committed to helping you achieve clear, healthy skin.
