Our Team

Dr. Sarah Johnson

MD, FAAD

Medical Director

Dr. Johnson is a board-certified dermatologist with over 15 years of experience in medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology. She completed her fellowship in Mohs Micrographic Surgery at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center and has performed over 10,000 skin cancer surgeries. Dr. Johnson is a fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology and serves on the editorial board of the Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology.
